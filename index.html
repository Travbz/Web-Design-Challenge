Summary

This site is a summary of how weather patterns change as your latitude changes with respect to the equator. Latitude and and temperature are strongly correlated. These graphs show that the Southern Hemisphere has higher temperatures than the Northern Hemisphere. This is most probably due to the analysis in the Northern Hemisphere being recorded in the winter and the amount of heat exchange occuring between the greater surface area of ocean in the Southern Hemisphere.

The measures of weather used are temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed. The weather data was taken from openweathermap.org.
